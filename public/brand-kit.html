Trademark & Usage
Read before using any DIYNEZA brand asset

Written authorization

Use of any DIYNEZA brand asset — the logo, wordmark, or name — must be authorized in writing. Downloading assets here does not constitute permission to use them publicly.

Permitted uses

You may use the assets when accurately referring to DIYNEZA products or services, in editorial/press context, or where we have given explicit written approval. On web pages, the name and logo should link to the official DIYNEZA site.

Prohibited uses

Do not use the assets to imply sponsorship, affiliation, or endorsement without authorization; do not modify, distort, recolor, or combine the logo with other marks; do not associate the brand with illegal or harmful activity.

Visual integrity

Always reproduce the logo faithfully — never alter colors, proportions, typography, or spacing, and always keep the minimum clear space.

Ownership

DIYNEZA brand assets are the exclusive property of DIYNEZA. All goodwill generated through their use belongs to DIYNEZA.
